Our Community

We’re a group of members committed to providing a safe, and comfortable space for anyone to try their hand at coding, and have some fun along the way. As our founding member nhcarrigan is committed to helping members of the freecodecamp community, as well as EddieHub by providing further support to any who need it. We have a staff of moderators who share this vision, come meet them in our server and check out some of their amazing projects! You don’t have to be interested in coding to join us, we often have rounds of fun on Among Us, and talk about a variety of topics. Our moderator team with nhcarrigan, work with the community to set goals and provide projects that everyone can contribute to, in order to help build a sense of community
